About Us

#define is a non-profit organisation which conducts technical and non-technical events across colleges and aims to give life to a coding culture that promotes learning that is motivated by a fascination for new technologies. #define Society was established on 4th of September in 2019, when the Former PayTM Vice President, Mr. Saurabh Jain, graced us with his presence and keeping in mind our vision, we discussed open education solutions that would create an impact on how the tech industry functions today.

What is Hackdefine?

HackDefine is #define’s official Hackathon which will be partnering with Major League Hacking (MLH) for the first time and aims to help newbies gain confidence to code as well as help those who are in the game already sharpen their skills. HackDefine intends to bring together learners and mentors and help the Hackers who join us for this one-of-a-kind event build a community, learn, code, and grow together, emphasizing the importance of open learning and the viability of open education solutions. We intend to provide a place where thoughts and ideas flow free.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is an invention marathon. Students come together to build cool software & hardware hacks over 24-48 hours. It’s very beginner friendly.
How much does it cost?

Nothing. Attending a hackathon is free.
Do I need to be a student to attend?

Yes. Only Students who are currently enrolled at a college/university or have graduated within the past 12 months are eligible to attend.
